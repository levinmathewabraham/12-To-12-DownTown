About

"Welcome to 12to12, the crown jewel of Downtown Hotel's culinary offerings. Located in the heart of Nagampadam, Kottayam, our restaurant is a haven for food lovers and families alike. Our philosophy is simple: to provide an exceptional dining experience that exceeds our guests' expectations.
At 12to12, we're passionate about serving up delicious, freshly prepared dishes that cater to all tastes. Our all-you-can-eat spread is a masterpiece of culinary innovation, featuring a wide range of vegetarian delights and other mouthwatering options. And with our picturesque outdoor seating area, you can savor every bite in style. We're open from 12pm to 12am, seven days a week, so whether you're in the mood for a leisurely lunch, a family dinner, or a late-night snack, we've got you covered. Our friendly staff, elegant ambiance, and commitment to quality make us the perfect destination for any occasion. Come and experience the 12to12 difference for yourself – we can't wait to welcome you!"
